How Its Done

Pomodoro Technique

Used to help focus.

A time-management method where you work in focused intervals, followed by a short break. After a few cycles, you take a longer break to recharge.

Progressive Overload

Used to increase focus endurance.

A training principle where you gradually increase the intensity of workouts by adding weight, reps, or resistance over time. This approach challenges the muscles continuously, promoting growth, endurance, and improved performance.

Accountability Tracking

Used to stay consistant.

A method of setting measurable goals and regularly monitoring progress to ensure commitments are met. It helps you stay focused, motivated, and responsible for achieving specific tasks or objectives.
